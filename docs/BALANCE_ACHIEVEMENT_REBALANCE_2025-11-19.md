# Achievement Balance Update - 2025-11-19

## Overview
Addressed player feedback regarding achievement progression speed. Previous balance allowed unlocking ~50% of achievements (19/36) in a single standard run, including most character classes.

## Changes

### Combat Achievements
- **Kill Streak**: Increased from 50 kills in 10s to **50 kills in 10s** (Wait, I kept it 50/10 but increased others. Let me check my changes).
  - Actually, I kept `kill_streak` at 50/10 but increased `on_fire` (10->25), `unstoppable` (20->50), `godlike` (30->100), `immortal` (50->250).
- **On Fire!**: 10 -> 25 kill streak
- **Unstoppable Force**: 20 -> 50 kill streak
- **Godlike!**: 30 -> 100 kill streak
- **Immortal**: 50 -> 250 kill streak

### Progression Achievements
- **Level Up!**: Level 10 -> **Level 20**
- **Star Collector**: 1000 -> **5000** XP orbs
- **Meta Star Collector**: 100 -> **500** meta stars
- **Cosmic Veteran**: 500k -> **2,000,000** total damage
- **Galactic Explorer**: 100k -> **500,000** distance
- **Trigger Happy**: 25k -> **100,000** projectiles

### Skill & Challenge Achievements
- **Untouchable**: 60s -> **120s** without damage
- **Tank Commander**: 3 mins -> **5 mins** without dodging
- **Speed Runner**: Level 15 -> **Level 25** in single run
- **Elite Hunter**: 10 -> **50** elite kills

### Character Unlocks (Significant Difficulty Increase)
- **Stormcaller (Storm Surge)**: Hit 6 -> **10** enemies with chain lightning
- **Inferno Juggernaut (Grim Harvest)**: 1500 -> **5000** burn damage
- **Crimson Reaver (Crimson Pact)**: Heal 300 -> **1000** HP via lifesteal
- **Void Warden (Event Horizon)**: 15k -> **50,000** damage in single run
- **Cybernetic Berserker (Edge Walker)**: Survive 45s -> **60s** at <30% HP
- **Phantom Striker (Ricochet Rampage)**: 75 -> **200** ricochet bounces
- **Nexus Architect (Orbital Master)**: 5 -> **8** orbitals at once
- **Nova Corsair (Split Shot Specialist)**: Draft Split Shot 4 -> **5** times

### Special Achievements
- **Critical Master**: 50 -> **200** crits
- **Chain Reaction**: 5 -> **8** enemies hit
- **Ricochet Master**: 3 -> **5** enemies hit

## Impact
These changes shift the achievement curve from "early game flood" to "mid-to-late game goals". Character unlocks now require specific, dedicated builds rather than happening accidentally during a good run.
